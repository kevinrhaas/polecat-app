Free and paid, together
Run any mix in one prompt — free open models (Llama, GPT-OSS, Qwen) right next to paid flagships (Opus, Gemini, GPT-5).
⇄Compare across providers — and the same family head-to-head, like Opus vs Sonnet.
$Spend smart. Explore with free models; bring in a paid one only where it counts.
Browse & search every model a provider offers, or pick from the curated list — add as many as you like.
You control the verdict
Consensus isn't a black box. Pick how it forms — Best, Comprehensive, Validated, or Debate — and which model gets the final say.
Example: let fast free models draft the research, then set Claude Opus as the arbiter for the final call — cheap exploration, premium judgment.
Or switch consensus off and just read every model side by side.
Yours, on your device
Every conversation is saved locally in the ☰ menu — revisit and continue any of them. Nothing leaves your browser.
Search, rename & pin — find any chat, retitle it, keep favorites on top.
Private mode for when you don't want a chat recorded.

Bring your own keys
Add at least one API key — free (OpenRouter, Groq, Hugging Face) or paid (Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT). Keys stay in your browser and go straight to each provider.
Free models cost $0; paid ones usually run pennies per question, and you set the limits. New to this? Settings → Keys explains what an API key is and what to expect to pay.
