You control the verdict

Customizable consensus

The combined answer isn't a black box. Choose how it's formed, name which model gets the final say, or turn it off to compare side by side.

  • Best Answer — pick the single strongest, lightly corrected.
  • Merge Everything — combine everything into the most complete answer.
  • Fact-Checked Merge — merge, but cross-check claims and flag disagreements.
  • Debate & Merge — models critique each other, then merge the best of each.
  • Refine Together — each model refines a running draft.

Edit the prompts, save your own strategies, and export/import your whole setup.

Private & yours

Bring your own key, and your API keys and your conversations live only in your browser — prompts go straight to each provider, never to us. No server, no accounts. Attached files (PDFs, Word docs, spreadsheets, images) are read entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere. It's a static site you could fork and run yourself. (Two exceptions: the free demo and the Polecat Model Server both run through a lightweight proxy so you can try Polecat without a key.)
